HR Leave Policy

Employees are eligible for annual paid leave, sick leave, and personal leave based on company rules. Leave requests must be submitted through the employee portal before the planned leave date. Managers review leave requests and may approve or reject them based on staffing needs and employee eligibility.

Sick Leave Policy

Employees may take sick leave when they are unable to work due to illness, medical appointments, or health-related reasons. For longer absences, the employee may be required to provide supporting documentation. Sick leave should be reported as soon as possible.

Attendance Policy

Employees are expected to follow their assigned work schedule. Repeated late arrivals, unexplained absences, or frequent early departures may be reviewed by the manager. Attendance records are tracked using check-in time, check-out time, work hours, late minutes, and overtime hours.

Payroll Policy

Payroll is processed based on the employee's salary, allowances, overtime, bonus, deductions, and tax amount. Net salary is calculated after applying deductions and taxes. Payroll records are confidential and should only be accessed by authorized HR or payroll staff.

Training Policy

Employees may be assigned training programs based on their role, department, skill gaps, or compliance requirements. Training status may be tracked as enrolled, in progress, completed, or cancelled. Training scores and certificate status may be used for employee development planning.

Performance Review Policy

Employees receive performance reviews based on goals, technical skills, communication, teamwork, leadership, and overall contribution. Review results may be used for development planning, promotion recommendations, and future goal setting.

Certification Policy

Professional certifications help validate employee skills. Employees may submit certifications with issue date, expiry date, issuing organization, and renewal status. Some certifications may require renewal before expiry.

Data Privacy Policy

Employee personal information, payroll information, performance reviews, and contact details must be handled securely. Access should be limited based on job role and business need. Sensitive employee data must not be shared with unauthorized users.
